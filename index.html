PURPOSE!!


India is a 'DEVELOPING' country and not 'DEVELOPED' if we want to change India into a developed country we need to focus on this major part of society. This major part includes different types of poor people for e.g.

  • Children till the age of 12 begging because they are not given any opportunity to study because there parents are poor,
  • people between the age of 15-35 who are unemployed because of two reasons either they are unqualified or if they are qualified then they are not offered jobs.
  • People older than that working as labours,begging etc.

HUMAAN will help this major part of the society by providing them opportunities like jobs,education,health care facilities,etc.and for that "WE NEED YOUR SUPPORT".
